N.J.A.C. 7:8 Stormwater Management Requirements

Stormwater Management Plan
  • Structural and non-structural strategies to meet stormwater goals (Page 12, "A stormwater management plan shall include structural and nonstructural...")
  • Safety standards for stormwater basins (Page 12, "A stormwater management plan shall incorporate the safety standards...")
  • Consideration of ecological and physical site characteristics (Page 12, "...shall consider the physical characteristics and ecological resources...")
  • Coordination with municipal or regional plans (Page 12, "...shall be coordinated with any other stormwater management plans...")
Groundwater Recharge & Runoff Quantity
  • Maintain 100% of annual pre-development recharge (Page 28, "...shall be designed to maintain 100 percent of the average annual pre-construction groundwater recharge...")
  • No increase in peak runoff for 2-, 10-, and 100-year storms (Page 29, "...shall be designed to reduce the post-construction peak flow rates... 50, 75, and 80 percent...")
Stormwater Runoff Quality
  • 80% TSS removal from average annual runoff (Page 30, "...shall be designed to reduce the post-construction load of total suspended solids (TSS) by 80 percent...")
